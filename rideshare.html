Rideshare Incentives

$2/day Incentive

New ridesharers who try carpooling, vanpooling, riding the bus or Metrolink, bicycling, walking, or telecommuting may be eligible to receive a $2/day incentive for each day they rideshare for the first three months.

Vanpool Incentive

Each new start-up vanpool originating and terminating in the Inland Empire may receive up to $1,800 over a 9-month period: $300 per month for the first 3 months, $200 per month for the next 3 months, and $100 per month for the last 3 months. Call 1-866-RIDESHARE to find out more.

RidesharePlus

If you are a Riverside County or San Bernardino County resident and carpool, vanpool, take the bus or Metrolink, bicycling, walk, or telecommute at least one day a week, you could be eligible. Your RidesharePlus membership entitles you to a coupon book full of savings and access to a members only website where you can print coupons from more than 135,000 merchants.

For more information or to find out if you are eligible for one or more of these incentives, call 1-866-RIDESHARE (1-866-743-3742).
